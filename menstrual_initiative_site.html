About Our Initiative

We are a student-led, grant-funded project distributing menstrual cups and culturally sensitive education at the Worcester Free Care Collaborative (WFCC). Our goal is to improve menstrual equity, reduce financial barriers, and promote sustainable period solutions.

Our Mission

To ensure that all women, regardless of income or insurance status, have access to safe, sustainable menstrual products and education. By providing reusable menstrual cups and alternative options, we aim to reduce stigma, empower women, and improve health outcomes.
